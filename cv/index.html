Latest publications

  • 2025.12.15
    Automatic Ability-Based Design (AutoABD): A Novel Framework for Accessible Technology Evaluated on Visual Abilities
    International Journal of Human–Computer Interaction
    Introduction of autoABD, a framework that automatically adapts to its users' abilities. autoABD's feasibility is demonstrated on users with and without vision impairment, where users with a vision impairment are correctly identified and equivalent accessibility is achieved.
  • 2025.10.21
    SIMBA: A robust and generalizable measure of data imbalance
    Patterns
    This paper critically reviews all existing imbalance measures and introduces the Status of IMBAlance (SIMBA), a robust and generalizable imbalance measure. Indeed, SIMBA can be used on datasets with any number of classes, features, and samples, and accurately reflects classification difficulty stemming from imbalanced data.
